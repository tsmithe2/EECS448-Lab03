A biography

My name is Harry Potter, and I am a wizard! I have spent several years studying magic at the university of Hogwarts. During my time there, many times, I was confronted by Voldemort. I managed to reduce him to ashes in my "seventh year". It wasn't really a true year of school, considering that I spent the whole time finding and destroying hoarcruxes! I fell in love with Jinny Weasly after finishing school at Hogwarts. We now have two kids, and life couldn't be any better! After all, there's no way Voldemort could possibly return. There isn't even another dark wizard out there to stir up any trouble. At least, I don't think there is.
